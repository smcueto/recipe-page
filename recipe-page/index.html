Caldo de Pollo (Chicken Soup)

It is a rainy day or maybe you are starting to show signs of a cold coming on. One of the best dishes to make to feel to relax and comforted. Not just any soup, easy savoury Caldo de Pollo AKA Chicken Soup!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 onion
  • 2 Cloves of garlic, minced
  • 3 Tablespoons of Salt
  • 4 Pounds of chicken breast
  • 4 Large potatoes of your choosing
  • 3 Medium Zucchini
  • 1 Bag of baby carrots
  • 1 Can of sweet corn
  • 2 Chayotes
  • 1/2 Bunch of fresh cilantro
  • 2 Gallons of water
  • 2 Limes
  • 2 Chiles Serranos
  • OPTIONAL: 1 cup of white rice
  • OPTIONAL: 1 bag of Spinach
