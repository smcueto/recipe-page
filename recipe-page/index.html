Caldo de Pollo (Chicken Soup)

It is a rainy day or maybe you are starting to show signs of a cold coming on. One of the best dishes to make to feel to relax and comforted. Not just any soup, easy savoury Caldo de Pollo AKA Chicken Soup!

Ingredients

Amount Item Notes
1 Onion
2 Cloves of garlic -
3 tbsp Salt -
4 lbs Chicken Breast -
4 Potatoes Large, any type
3 Zucchini Medium Size
1 bag Baby Carrots Medium Size
1 can Sweet Yellow Corn Can use whole corn instead.
2 Chayotes Medium Size
1/2 bunch Cilantro -
2 gal Water -
2 Limes -
2 Bay Leaves -
2 Chiles Serranos Optional
1 cup White Rice Optional
1 bag Spinach Optional

Prepare Ingredients

  • Make sure all veggies and chicken have been cleaned under under cold water.
  • Mince Garlic into small pieces or put through garlic press.
  • Chop chicken breast into cubes.
  • Chop Zucchini into medium cubes or half slices.
  • Chop Union into four large cubes.
  • Chop Chayotes into four large cubes.
  • Cut Limes in four ways.
  • Finely chop all cilantro.
  • Optional: Slice Chiles Serranos into 6 rows.

Cooking Soup Instructions

  1. Bring 2 gallons of water to a boil in a large pot.
  2. While water is boiling, add chicken cubes and unnion into pot and let it cook for 10 mins. If foam forms, scoop out of pot and dispose.
  3. Add minced garlic, bay leaves, and baby carrots to boiling pot with chicken. Carrots take the longest to cook, this must be put in first. Let cook until carrots begin to get soft. Estimation of 20-25 mins.
  4. once Carrots are getting soft, add salt, Zucchini, Chayotes, and sweet corn and let cook for another 10-15 mins or until veggies begin getting soft. Optional, you can add Spinach.
  5. Taste broth and make sure it is salted to desired amount. Check if veggies are soft enough to serve, if not, leave in pot for another 5-10 mins or until desired texture.
  6. Optional: Boil white rice in pot of water until cooked.
  7. Serve on large bowl and add cilantro, lime,and optional serrano and white rice to your bowl.
  8. Enjoy!
